## 1. Creation Reimagined

**Speaker:**
We started with the screen you use most: your phone.
Most survey tools are built for desktops. Sight is built for your hand.

We’ve stripped away the clutter.
*   **Intuitive Builder:** Add questions, set types, reorder. It just flows.
*   **Instant Publishing:** One tap, and your survey is live to the world.

It works like magic. You don’t manage data; you create knowledge.

## 2. Participation that Pays

**Speaker:**
Now, how do you get answers?
Usually, you have to beg.
With Sight, people *want* to answer.

We’ve built a **Rewards System** right into the core.
*   **Earn Points:** Every answer gives you points.
*   **Level Up:** Build your streak. Rise in status.
*   **Seamless Interface:** No more clunky web forms. It’s as smooth as your favorite social feed.

It’s not a chore. It’s a game. And it’s fun.
